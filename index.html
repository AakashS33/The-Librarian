BOTW Fiction Ready Player Two

This weeks book discussion features Ready Player Two which is a 2020 science fiction novel by American author Ernest Cline. It is the sequel to his 2011 debut novel Ready Player One. Ready Player Two was published and released on November 24, 2020. It debuted at number one on The New York Times Best Seller list.
